Scholarship & Aid
Students of the University are eligible to receive all Scholarships and Grants constituted by the State and Central Governments. All scholarships and concession in fees are subject to the good conduct and satisfactory progress of the beneficiaries. Certain concessions in fees in the forms of granting full free studentship, half-free studentship, quarter-free studentship are awarded to meritorious students, who may be in indigent circumstances. Stipends and fee waivers are available for BPL students.

Please contact the Admissions department to know more about scholarships and aid.
